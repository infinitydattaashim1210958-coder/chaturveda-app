❓ Help & FAQ

What is Chaturveda?

Chaturveda is a digital platform for reading and exploring Vedic texts, Sanskrit literature, and related knowledge resources.

Is Chaturveda free?

Yes, Chaturveda is designed to provide accessible Vedic knowledge resources.

Can I read texts offline?

Offline reading availability depends on downloaded content and app features.

How can I suggest new features?

You can send suggestions through the Feedback section.

Where do the texts come from?

Texts are collected from traditional, public-domain, and scholarly sources.

How can I report an error?

Use the Report a Bug section inside the app.
